About

Hello, I'm Stefania.

I'm an Illustrator and Concept Artist with professional experience in mobile game development. My work spans character design, prop concepts, promotional art, and visual development — from early thumbnail explorations to fully rendered game-ready assets.

I've contributed character art, environmental assets, and marketing illustrations for published mobile titles including Firestone. I'm equally at home designing stylized creatures and mechs, exploring fan concepts, or developing original IP from scratch.
